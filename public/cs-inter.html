CS Intermediate Course

There are seven subjects for CS Intermediate (Executive), carrying 100 marks each divided into two modules as module Ⅰ and module Ⅱ



The following are the subjects of CS Intermediate (Executive)

    Module Ⅰ


    Paper 1- Company Law

    Paper 2 –Cost and Management Accounting

    Paper 3 – Economics and Commercial Laws

    Paper 4 – Tax Laws and Practice


    Module Ⅱ


    Paper 5 – Company Accountants and Auditing Practices

    Paper 6– Capital Market and Security laws

    Paper 7– Industrial, Labor, General Laws

    • The students can appear both the Module 1 and Module 2 at a time or single module.
    • The students should score 40% in each paper and 50% in aggregate



    Rules for Registration



    •The CS Foundation (CSEET) qualified candidates are eligible for the CS Intermediate (Executive)

    • Graduates except from Fine Arts

    • CA and CMA completed students


    • Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e., in June and December. To apply for June exam students should register before 30 th September and to apply for December exam registration should be done before 31 st march i.e., there must be a gap of 9 months between the Registration and Examination.

    • Registration fees (Both Modules/Single Module): 10000 RS

    • Examination fee for single Module: 1200

    • Examination fee for both Modules: 2400

    • For SC/ST and handicapped concession 50%

    • Last date for payment of exam fee is 25 th march for June exam and for December exam
    September 30 th i.e., there must be a gap of approximately 2 months between the payment of
    exam fee and exams.


    Rules for Articleship


    For 21 months Articleship, the student should complete both the group. It can be done even after final.
