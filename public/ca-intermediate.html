CA Intermediate Course

There are 8 subjects for CA inter, carrying 100 marks each divided in to 2 groups as Group-Ⅰ and Group -Ⅱ



There are four subjects for CA Intermediate

    GROUP-Ⅰ


    PAPER 1 – Accounting
    PAPER 2 – Business Laws and other Laws
    PAPER 3 – Cost and Management Accounting
    PAPER 4 – Taxation


    GROUP Ⅱ

    PAPER 5 –Advanced Accounting
    PAPER 6 – Auditing and Assurance
    PAPER 7 – Enterprise Information System and Strategic Management
    PAPER 8 – Financial Management and Economics for Finance



The students can appear both the Group Ⅰ and Group Ⅱ at a time or single group
The students should score 40% in each paper and 50% in aggregate



Rules for Registration


•The CA foundation qualified candidates are eligible for the CA intermediate course

• Students with graduation or post-graduation in commerce with at least 55% marks can apply
directly for CA Intermediate

• Students from Non-Commerce disciplines at the graduate level, can also apply directly in case they
have at least 60% marks for their degree course

• The students who have passed the intermediate level from the Institute of Company Secretary of
India or Institute of Cost Accounts of India

• Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e., every May and November. To appear for May exam
registration should be done before October 30 th and for November exam registration should be done
before30 th April. There must be a minimum gap of 6 months between the registration and
examination

• Registration fees for both groups -18000

• Registration fees for single group-11000

• CA Inter course registration is valid for 4 years and can be revalidated any number of times for
further 4 years. Once the initial registration is over candidate can have to revalidate their
registration by paying a revalidation fees RS 400 or as decided by the council from time to time.

• Exam fee for both group -2700

• Exam fee for single group -1500

• Last date for payment of Exam fee is March13 th for May Exam and November Exam is September 15.
There must be a minimum gap of 45days between the course and payment of exam fee.


Rules for Articleship


The students who have passed any of the groups at intermediate level can start the 3 years of
Articleship with a registered Chartered Accountant. After 2 1/2 years of Articleship he can write final
exam and balance can be done even after final.
