CMA Foundation Course

There are 4 Subjects for CMA Foundation each paper carries 100 marks

    Paper 1-Fundamentels of Economics and Management

    Paper 2-Fundamentals of Accounting

    Paper 3- Fundamentals of Laws and Ethics

    Paper 4- Fundamentals of Business Mathematics and Ethics



    All the exams should be cleared in one sitting. Minimum pass marks in each paper 40% and minimum pass marks in aggregate 50%.Types of questions are Objective cum Subjective. All the exams are online mode.

    Rules for Registration


    1. Students who have cleared their +2 or other equivalent course from a recognized
    Institution are eligible to attend their CMA Foundation.
    2. The students who have passed 10 th class can also register for CMA Foundation and to
    get a provisional registration.
    3. Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e., June and December. Last date of registration
    before January 31st for June and July 31 st for December exam. There must be a
    minimum gap of four months between the registration and examination to be
    appeared.
    4. Registration fees (6000 including Exam Fee)
    5. The CMA Foundation course registration is valid for 3years and no revalidation
