    Rules for Registration


    •To register for CS final (professional) the students should pass all the three modules of CS INTER
    (Executive)


    •Registration fees (All Modules/ single Module)-12000 RS

    •Exams are conducted for CS Final twice in a year i.e., in June and December. For June exams, registration
    should be done before September 30 th and for December exam March 31th.To apply for all modules of
    the exam after registration at least 9 months of gap and for one modules 6 months gap.

    •Exam fee for all the modules: 3600

    •Exam fee for single modules: 1200

    •For SC/ST, handicapped for 50% concession

    •For June exam, exam fee should be paid before April 15 and December exam October 15.There must be a
    gap of at least 45 days before exams and payment of exam fee.
