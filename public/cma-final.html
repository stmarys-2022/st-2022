CMA Final Course

There are 8 subjects for CMA final carrying 100marks each, divided into 2 Groups as Group Ⅰ and group Ⅱ



The following are the subject of CMA Final

    Group-1

    Paper 13-Corporate Law and Compliance

    Paper14 –Strategic Finical Management

    Paper15-Stratagic Cost Management –Decision Making


    Paper16-Direct Tax Laws and International Taxation


    Group-2


    Paper17-Corporate Finical Reporting

    Paper18-Indirect Tax Laws and Practice

    Paper19-Cost and Management Audit

    Paper20-Stratagic Performance Management and Business Validation


    The students should complete an Articleship of 15 months, before getting their final exam results. The students can start Articleship immediately after registering for Inter. The students should score 40%in each paper and 50% in aggregate.



    Rules for Registration


    •To register for CMA final the students should have passed both group of CMA Inter.

    •Registration for CMA final exam (Single/both) is RS25000.

    •Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e., June and December. To apply for June exam registration should
    be done before January31 and for December exam the Registration should be done before July 31.

    •There must be a minimum gap of 4 months between registration and examination.
