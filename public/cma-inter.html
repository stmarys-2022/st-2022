CMA Intermediate Course

There are eight subjects for CMA Inter, carrying 100 Marks each divided into two groups as Group- Ⅰ and group-Ⅱ



The following are the subjects of CMA Intermediate

    Group-Ⅰ

    Paper5: Financial Accounting

    Paper 6: Law &amp; Ethics

    Paper 7: Direct Taxation

    Paper 8: Cost Accounting

    Group-Ⅱ

    Paper 9: Operations Management &amp; Strategic Management

    Paper 10: Cost and Management Accounting and Financial Management

    Paper 11: Indirect Taxation

    Paper 12: Company Accounts and Audit


    The students can appear both the group 1 and group 2 at a time or single group The Students should score 40% in each paper and 50% in aggregate


    Rules for Registration


    The admission in the CMA Intermediate Course can take place by four means

    • The CA foundation qualified candidates are eligible for the CMA intermediate course

    • Graduation in any subjects excluding Fine Arts

    • Foundation part 1 Examination and combinatory level part2 examination of CAT at the institute

    • Foundation part1 Examination of the CAT of the institute


    • Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e. June and December. To appear for June exam the
    registration should be done before July 31 st and for December exam the registration should be
    done before July 31 st. There must be a minimum gap 4 months between the registration and
    examination

    • Registration fees (Single/Both)- RS 25100

    • Examination fee for Single group-1200

    • Examination fee for both group – 2400

    • Last date of payment of Exam fee is 10 th October for December exam and June exam is April 15.

    •They must be a minimum gap of 45 days between the exams and payment of exam fee.


    Rules for Articleship


    The students should complete Articleship of 15 months; he should complete both the group to start Articleship. He can write final exam after completing 10 months of Articleship and balance can be done even after final.
