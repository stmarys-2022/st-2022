CS Foundation Course

Study CS foundation course (CSEET) with St. Mary’s coaching center to get good knowledge and results



CS Foundation has Four Subjects

    Paper1- Business Communication (50marks)


    Paper2- Legal Aptitude and Logical Reasoning (50marks)

    Paper3- Economic and Business Environment (50marks)

    Paper4-Current Affairs (20marks)


    All the exams should be cleared in one sitting. Minimum pass mark in each paper 40% and minimum pass mark in aggregate 50%.The mode of exam is computer based i.e., all the exams are online. All subjects are objective type.



    Rules for Registration


    •Students who have cleared their +2 or other equivalent course from a recognized Institution
    are eligible to attend their CS Foundation.

    • The students who have passed 10 th class can also register for CS foundation and to get a
    provisional registration

    • Exams are conducted twice in a year i.e., June and December. To apply for June exams
    registration should be done before December 17 and for December exam October 15 i.e.,
    there must be a minimum gap of 45 days between the registration and the examination

    • Registration fees-1000 (including Exam Fee)

    • For SC/ST and Handicapped 50% concession
