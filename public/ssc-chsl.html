SSC CHSL (Combined Higher secondary level)

Basic Qualification: 12 th qualified candidates This exam is held in 3-phases as below


Tier-1(online)

Subjects

    • English Language (25 questions)

    • General Intelligence (25 questions)

    • Quantitative Aptitude (25 questions)

    • General Awareness (25 questions)

    Each question carries 2 marks and there is a ¼ negative marking associated with every wrong answer.

    After completing tier 1, the students should attend tier 2.


    The followings are subjects of Tier 2(Descriptive)


    Subjects


    • English/ Hindi (optional)

    • Essay writing

    • Letter writing

    • Application writing

    After clearing tier-2, the student should attend tier 3.
    The following are the subjects of Tier -3

    Subjects


    • Skill test

    • Typing test

    Working Hours

    9AM TO 9PM (Including Sundays)
