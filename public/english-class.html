GENERAL INTRODUCTION ABOUT ENGLISH CLASSES

It is the dream of the students to go abroad for Higher Studies or Jobs. To go aboard for these purposes, they have to pass certain English Language Courses such as IELTS (International English Language Testing System) and fluent in English. Join St. Mary’s Coaching Centre for IELTS, SPOKEN ENGLISH and GRAMMAR classes to fulfill your dreams.


There are two different types of IELTS, namely


1) IELTS – Academic
2) IELTS – General



    IELTS Syllabus contains four sections, namely


    • Reading

    • Writing

    • Speaking

    • Listening

    For Academic and General IELTS Reading and Writing are different .But, for Academic and General IELTS listening and speaking are the same. If you want to move an English speaking country to study purpose, you should take IELTS Academic test. For work or immigration purpose , IELTS General is recommended.

    EXAM PATTERN


    • Speaking -3 Questions 11-14 minutes

    • Listening-2 recordings 40 questions 30+10minutes

    • Writing -2 questions 60 minutes

    • Reading-40 questions 60 minutes


    PASS MARK


    There is no pass or fail in IELTS. IELTS result is calculated from BAND 1 to 9 (highest).There is a BAND 0 scores as well for those who had not attempted the test. The increments are given in half Bands.
