General Introduction


St. Mary’s Coaching Centre was established in the year 2003 at North Paravur in Kerala as its Head Office. We have branches in Kerala at Aluva and Kodungallur and are planning to open more branches in Kerala and other states. Our 20 years of dedicated service and the best performance of our students in their exams and professions brought us to the forefront of the top most institutes in India. Initially, we started our institute as a Commerce Professional Coaching Center giving coaching for CA, CS and CMA. After the successful completion of many batches of the above courses, due to heavy pressure and requests from the students, parents and public at large, we introduced the competitive exams coaching also in our institute. It was noticed that many students could not perform well in their examinations and interviews because of their substandard English. With a view to improve their English, we also started English language courses such as IELTS, SPOKEN ENGLISH & GRAMMAR. It is a fact that many students are weak in some particular subjects irrespective of their high scoring in all the other subjects. To identify the areas of their weakness and to make them strong in those areas, we also started individual tuition classes in all the important subjects of various courses. St. Mary’s Coaching Centre is the only one coaching Centre which brings the study of various courses under one roof. This helps the students to prepare for various examinations simultaneously and to save the time and money and makes their studies easier. Our fully dedicated management and team of highly qualified and experienced teachers are always at your service.
